Loop Break is a daily racing game. Every UTC midnight, a brand-new track is generated for every player on the planet — learn the corners, beat your best lap, and climb the global leaderboard before it resets. When you're not chasing the daily, Endurance Mode keeps your car lapping in the background, banking Credits to upgrade your garage one lap at a time.

Two Modes, One Pursuit

[01]

Daily Race

A fresh procedurally generated track every 24 hours — the same track for every driver on Earth. Set the fastest lap and climb the global leaderboard. Race a faint ghost of your own best lap to hunt your PB corner by corner, or jump on as a Guest with no profile required.

[02]

Endurance Mode

An idle companion to the daily grind. Your car keeps lapping while you're away, banking Credits even offline. Spend them on garage upgrades, automation, and sponsors to grow your racing team one lap at a time.
