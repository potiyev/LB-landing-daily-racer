Loop Break is a daily racing game. Every UTC midnight, a brand-new track is generated for every player on the planet — you get one shot to set your best time, then climb the global leaderboard. When the daily run isn't enough, drop into the Sprint Gauntlet rogue-lite mode for five back-to-back tracks, picking powers and upgrades along the way.

Two Modes, One Pursuit

[01]

Daily Time Trial

A fresh procedurally generated track every 24 hours. Same track for every driver on Earth. One run. Set the fastest lap and climb the global leaderboard. Miss a day? Your streak resets.

[02]

Sprint Gauntlet

A rogue-lite chain of five escalating tracks. Pick from 7 powers — Nitro, Grip Lock, Ghost Mode, Time Warp, Shield, Off-Road Recovery, Racing Line. Three driver archetypes. Earn Nitro to unlock permanent upgrades.
