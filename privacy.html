Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-05-23

This policy explains how Loop Break — Daily Driver handles your data on iOS, Android, and the web. The short version: we don't run ads, we don't use analytics, and we don't share your data with anyone.

The Simple Version

Loop Break is built privacy-first. We only collect what we absolutely need to make the game work — basically, your nickname and lap times for the global leaderboard. Everything else lives on your device.

What We DON'T Collect

  • We don't show ads, so we don't share data with ad networks
  • We don't run any analytics or telemetry SDK
  • We don't track you across apps or websites
  • We don't collect your real name, email, phone number, or address
  • We don't access your contacts, photos, microphone, or precise location
  • We don't sell your data — to anyone, ever

Online Leaderboards & Profiles

When you submit a time to the global leaderboard, we send the following to our backend (hosted on Supabase, a managed PostgreSQL provider):

  • Your in-game nickname (you choose this — make it whatever you want)
  • Your lap time and the seed of the daily track you ran
  • Your country code (derived from your device locale, not your IP)
  • An anonymous device identifier we generate locally — not your IDFA or advertising ID

That's it. We don't store your IP address, your email, or any account credentials. If you never play online, none of this leaves your device.

What Stays On Your Device

The following data is stored locally on your phone (or your browser) and never leaves it unless you explicitly choose to play online:

  • Your game progress — Sprint Gauntlet runs, unlocked upgrades, Nitro balance
  • Your settings — audio levels, haptics, control preferences, theme
  • Your local personal-best times
  • Your Game Center / Google Play Games handle (managed by Apple / Google, not us)

Uninstalling the app deletes all of this. There is nothing in the cloud to migrate.

Children's Privacy

Loop Break is not directed at children under 13. We don't knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has submitted data, contact us and we'll remove it.

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your data with any third party. The only outbound data transfer is to Supabase (our database host), which stores leaderboard records on our behalf. Supabase is bound by its own privacy policy and contracted as a data processor only.

What You Can Control

  • Skip online play entirely — the game is fully playable offline
  • Reset your online profile from in-app settings at any time
  • Disable Game Center / Play Games sign-in in your device settings
  • Uninstall the app to wipe all local data

Changes to This Policy

If we ever update this policy, we'll update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and post a notice in-app before any material change takes effect. We will never expand our data collection without telling you first.
